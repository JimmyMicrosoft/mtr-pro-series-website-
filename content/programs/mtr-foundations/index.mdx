## About this program

MTR Foundations is the entry point for every AV integrator working with Microsoft Teams Rooms. It covers the three pillars every deployment depends on: understanding the platform and ecosystem, executing a professional physical installation, and configuring devices correctly the first time.

The program is designed for learners with general AV integration experience who are new to Microsoft Teams Rooms — and for experienced integrators who want to formalize and verify their knowledge against a structured curriculum.
