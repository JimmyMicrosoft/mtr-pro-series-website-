## Overview

A professional MTR installation begins before anyone picks up a drill. The preparation work — surveying the site, assessing infrastructure, producing the bill of materials, and understanding the safety requirements — determines whether the physical installation goes smoothly or generates expensive surprises. Integrators who invest in thorough pre-installation planning arrive on-site knowing exactly what they need, where everything goes, and what they might encounter. Integrators who skip this step spend installation days solving problems that should have been identified in the planning phase.

This module covers the four planning pillars that precede physical installation: site documentation, infrastructure readiness, material procurement, and safety compliance.

---

## What you will learn

By completing this module, you will be able to:

- Conduct a structured AV room survey capturing all physical, architectural, and infrastructure data required for accurate system design
- Assess electrical infrastructure (circuit capacity, UPS requirements), network infrastructure (bandwidth, PoE, VLAN, QoS), and AV pathway infrastructure (conduit, cable tray, access)
- Produce a complete, accurate BOM with certified hardware, cable quantities, and all consumables — plus a delivery and staging schedule that accounts for lead times
- Identify the safety regulations and codes applicable to commercial AV installation and apply lockout/tagout procedures correctly
