## Overview

Enterprise MTR deployments are not managed one device at a time. At scale — dozens or hundreds of rooms across multiple sites — manual approaches break down. This track covers the Microsoft 365 administration skills that enable fleet-scale management: scripted resource account provisioning, policy-based device management through Teams Admin Center, and the Teams Rooms Pro Management platform for proactive monitoring, incident management, and remote operations.

---

## What you will learn

By completing this track, you will be able to:

- Provision MTR resource accounts in bulk using PowerShell, including license assignment, calendar processing configuration, and Entra ID attribute management
- Design and implement Conditional Access policies that secure the MTR device estate without blocking Teams Rooms sign-in
- Manage TAC device configuration profiles, meeting policies, and update windows at fleet scale
- Use the Teams Rooms Pro Management portal for proactive incident detection, automated alerting, remote device management, and utilization analytics
- Build repeatable governance frameworks for naming conventions, license optimization, and ongoing resource account lifecycle management
