## Overview

Teams Admin Center is the operational control plane for the MTR fleet. Used effectively, it provides centralized visibility, policy management, and configuration deployment without requiring physical access to individual devices. Used poorly — or not used at all — it leaves the fleet in an unmanaged state where devices drift out of policy compliance, run outdated firmware, and develop issues that go undetected until a meeting fails. This module covers the TAC management capabilities that keep a fleet of any size under consistent, proactive control.

---

## What you will learn

By completing this module, you will be able to:

- Use TAC's tagging, filtering, and bulk action capabilities to manage large device inventories efficiently
- Create and assign configuration profiles that enforce device settings across assigned rooms without manual file management
- Configure Teams meeting policies that optimize the meeting experience for room devices
- Set update policies with maintenance windows that prevent disruptive updates during business hours
- Monitor device health at fleet scale and investigate devices showing health warnings
