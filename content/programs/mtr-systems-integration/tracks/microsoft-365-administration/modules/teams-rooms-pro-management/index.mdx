## Overview

Teams Rooms Pro Management is a dedicated portal for organizations and service providers who need more than TAC's basic health monitoring. It provides automated incident detection with AI-powered analysis, remote device operations, granular meeting quality data, and utilization analytics — all in a portal designed around the managed services workflow. For integrators delivering ongoing managed services, Pro Management is the operational hub that makes proactive monitoring at scale practical.

---

## What you will learn

By completing this module, you will be able to:

- Navigate the Pro Management portal and understand its relationship to TAC
- Interpret automated incident tickets and use the built-in investigation tools to identify root causes
- Perform remote management operations — reboot, log collection, and device commands — from the portal
- Pull and present utilization and quality analytics data for client reporting
- Structure a managed services operation around Pro Management as the monitoring backbone
